About Aidan

I’m a Pacific Northwest photographer drawn to the tension between stillness and power — the moment a storm breaks over a ridgeline, the quiet intensity of a wild animal, the unguarded truth between two people. Rooted in Bellingham, Washington, I work across the full breadth of the PNW and travel wherever the story demands. My approach blends technical precision with instinct for light, timing, and presence.
